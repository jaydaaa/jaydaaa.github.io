Evaluation of Web Graphics

Site Evaluated: BuzzFeed

Purpose of graphics on this site

I feel that the purposes of the graphics included in the website are used to commnicate previews of Buzzfeed's articles they provide. In addition, I find it appealing to see a sneak peak into their topics because it makes it more efficient to solve what the audience wants to indulge in. While additionally adding other graphics into the website without making the page ddistracting or overbearing.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. Yes - Graphics fit in with the purpose, organization, and style of the page.
  2. Yes - Large Graphics have been avoided.
  3. No - The graphics don't help to guide the viewer's focus to the important content on the page.
  4. Yes - Overly bright and potentially "obnoxious" images have been avoided.
  5. No - Graphics have been used for textual content, other than the banner.
  6. Yes - ALternate text is present for all images.
  7. No - Where text appears in graphics (the banner) there is no ample contrast.
